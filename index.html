About

I am a Research Scientist at Meta AI and Systems Codesign. I completed my PhD at Seoul National University, where I worked with Prof. Jung Ho Ahn, and was a visiting researcher at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with Prof. Nam Sung Kim from March 2023 to March 2024.

My current research focuses on improving GPU efficiency for Large Language Model and Latent Diffusion Model inference, including power efficiency, high-performance kernels, KV cache offloading and prefix caching across CPU memory, disaggregated memory, and SSD. I am also interested in maximizing sparsity in model weights and activations, as well as improving HBM architecture and the memory hierarchy of GPU systems.
